Understanding Anxiety

LA normal emotional and physical reaction to stress, imagined danger, or uncertainty is anxiety. It is typified by emotions of anxiety, fear, or trepidation, frequently accompanied by physical manifestations such as tenseness in the body, perspiration, fast breathing, or elevated heart rate. Although everyone experiences anxiety occasionally, severe or persistent anxiety can interfere with day-to-day functioning and may necessitate assistance..
