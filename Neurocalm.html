Breathing Exercises

Practice deep breathing to calm your mind and body Methods of Breathing Deep diaphragmatic breathing: By triggering the parasympathetic nervous system, deep, slow breathing can lower tension and foster serenity. 4-7-8 respiration: Take a 4-second breath, hold it for 7 seconds, and then let it out for 8 seconds. This promotes mental calmness and reduces anxiety.

Guided Meditation

Guided meditation is a powerful practice that uses focused relaxation techniques to calm the mind and body. It is typically conducted by a guide or instructor, either in person or through recordings. It entails being gently guided through a sequence of visualizations, breathing exercises, and mindfulness practices that help you focus, relax, and achieve inner peace..
